About PHK Ventures

PHK Ventures is the strategic investment arm under PHK Global, focusing on high-return, high-impact projects across robotics, fusion energy, space industry, financial infrastructure, and next-generation Physical AI.

Why We Invest

The reason is simple: Profit.
